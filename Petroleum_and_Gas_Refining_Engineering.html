Petroleum and Gas Refining

Department

Preparing and qualifying a new generation of graduates of oil engineers who are distinguished and specialized in practicing the profession to meet the needs of the growing labor market through the implementation of educational curricula and training programs and scientific excellence using educational techniques according to approved academic standards and highly qualified teaching staff and conducting studies and research Applied and provide engineering consultancy in all fields of oil and gas industry.

Mission

The preparation of an efficient and highly qualified generation of oil engineers capable of working in Oil Refineries, Petrochemicals and all the oil and gas industry facilities in the public and private sector companies, keeping abreast of the rapid global progress in this vital field and preparing researches and applied studies in all fields of oil and gas industry.

Vision

  • Preparing engineers with high technical level to work in Oil refining natural gas.
  • Developing laboratories and educational and training units to bring theoretical and engineering information closer to practical reality.
  • Giving graduate engineers sufficient information to enable them to operate or supervise the operation of industrial units for oil refineries and petrochemical and petrochemical plants.
  • Cooperation with the corresponding departments in the universities of the Ministry of Higher Education and Scientific Research to develop scientific curricula and benefit from the curricula of international universities in this field.
  • Cooperation with international and local companies to train students and make them qualified to work in the oil refineries and petrochemical field.

FAQ

What is the average grade required for acceptance?

71.5 for morning study, 64.5 for evening study.

What are the branches accepted by the department?

Biology graduates, Graduates of the applied science branch, Graduates of the scientific branch of previous years and those born to students from 1990 up for morning studies.

What is the corresponding department in government colleges?

The department is twinned with the University of Technology - Department of Chemical Engineering - Oil and Gas Refining Branch

Is there evening study in the department?

Yes, there is an evening study, and admission to it is not included in the age requirement.

How much is the tuition fee?

3,500,000 three million five hundred thousand Iraqi dinars.

Is the department recognized by the Ministry of Higher Education and Scientific Research?

Yes, all departments of Al-Farabi University College are permanently recognized and listed in the admission guide for private colleges issued by the ministry.
