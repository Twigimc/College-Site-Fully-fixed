Optics Technologies

Department

Qualifying the graduate in optical techniques and working in eye hospitals and health centers in a distinct and efficient manner. Using the latest technology in training and practical application Raise the quality of teaching optics techniques to develop students' skills and ensure access to knowledge by following and innovating the best methods and programs of study and teaching.

Mission

The department aspires to be a pioneer "in the field of technical education at the level of Iraq locally and internationally" and in the academic and professional fields. The department aspires to deepen the professional and trade union affiliation in its outputs. His outputs should be distinguished in theory and practice by providing the best knowledge programs in the field of optics technology. Adopting creative and innovative ideas, projects, and plans to improve optical instruction. Focusing on the practical aspect of education, so that its outputs are leaders in the field of optics.

Vision

  • Examination and correction of vision and determination of the degree of vision.
  • Repairing glasses and paying attention to medical and optical devices and how to take care of them
  • Fitting lenses for prescription glasses.
  • Prescribing eyeglasses and contact lenses.
  • The use of computers in the processes of checking and correcting eyesight.

FAQ

What is the average grade required for acceptance?

65 for morning study, 63 for evening study.

What are the branches accepted by the department?

Graduates of the biology branch, Graduates of the applied science and Graduates of the scientific branch from previous years and those born to students from 1990 up for morning studies.

What is the corresponding department in government colleges?

The department is twinned with College of Health and Medical Technologies - Baghdad / Department of Optometry

Is there evening study in the department?

Yes, there is an evening study, and admission to it is not included in the age requirement.

How much is the tuition fee?

2,750,000 two million and seven hundred and fifty thousand Iraqi dinars.

Is the department recognized by the Ministry of Higher Education and Scientific Research?

Yes, all departments of Al-Farabi University College are permanently recognized and listed in the admission guide for private colleges issued by the ministry.
