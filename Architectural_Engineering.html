Architectural Engineering

Department

Architecture Dept. of FUC seeks to qualify specialized engineers to meet the growing needs of the labor market through the implementation of educational curricula and training programs, using scientific distinctive educational techniques and staff of efficiency. The department is also kept to do scientific practical and applicable researches to serve environment and community.

Mission

The department of architecture is one on the distinctive department Its program aims to develop students understanding to interact between environment and human heredity factors, and link it with design process. Therefore, the program will give students a delicate sense for local and global engineering architecture, especially for civil planning and design.

Vision

  • Prepare scientifically qualified graduates who are able to contribute in serving community in the field of architectural schematic.
  • Stand for solving the problems that community suffers in the fields of architecture, planning, and housing by making appropriate researches and scientific studies.
  • Participate in scientific symposia and conferences specialized in architecture at the local and international level to upgrade the level of workers.
  • Establish mutual relations with other architectural department and associations.
  • Provide technical and practical consultancy sectors; private and government.

FAQ

What is the average grade required for acceptance?

69.5

What are the branches accepted by the department?

Graduates of the applied science branch for this year and last year, graduates of the scientific branch for previous years, and those born in 1990 and up

What is the corresponding department in government colleges?

The department is twinned with the University of Baghdad - Department of Architectural Engineering

Is there evening study in the department?

No, there isn’t

How much is the tuition fee?

The annual installment is four million Iraqi dinars.

Is the department recognized by the Ministry of Higher Education and Scientific Research?

Yes, all departments of Al-Farabi University College are permanently recognized and listed in the admission guide for private colleges issued by the ministry.
