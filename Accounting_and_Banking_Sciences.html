Accounting and Banking

Department

One of the main elements of the mission of the accounting department is to work to meet one of the most important goals on which the college was based, which is to qualify the student academically and scientifically in a manner that is fully consistent with the requirements of the labor market in particular and directly. As well as working to provide a distinguished educational environment, and for professors and teachers to conduct scientific research and provide services to the community by providing financial and accounting cadres and providing them in the various departments of the state.

Mission

Our vision stems from our knowledge of the requirements of society and the labor market that need such specialization, which serves all sectors of the state and which no institution in society is devoid of it and for both the public and private sectors.

Vision

  • Graduate scientific cadres specialized in accounting sciences.
  • Establish accounting courses for all government departments
  • Providing state departments with scientific cadres specialized in the field of accounts

FAQ

What is the average grade required for acceptance?

Unspecified grade average for both morning and evening study

What are the branches accepted by the department?

Graduates of the literary branch, Graduates of the biology branch, Graduates of the applied science, Graduates of the scientific branch and Commercial branch graduates (corresponding major only) from previous years and those born to students from 1990 up for morning studies.

What is the corresponding department in government colleges?

The department is twinned with Department of Accounting Sciences at the College of Business Economics / Al-Nahrain University.

Is there evening study in the department?

Yes, there is an evening study, and admission to it is not included in the age requirement.

How much is the tuition fee?

2,000,000 two million Iraqi dinars.

Is the department recognized by the Ministry of Higher Education and Scientific Research?

Yes, all departments of Al-Farabi University College are permanently recognized and listed in the admission guide for private colleges issued by the ministry.
