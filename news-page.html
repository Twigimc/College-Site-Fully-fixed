Blood donation campaign under the slogan of our blood and hearts with you

On Wednesday morning, 26/12/2018, the Student Activities Unit at Al-Farabi University College, in cooperation with the Ministry of Health and Environment, at the City of Medicine / Iraqi National Blood Bank organized a blood donation campaign for the Iraqi army, Al Hashed Forces and the children of cancer patients. This activity comes to strength the role of the college, its students, its teachers and its staff towards the society and to support the humanitarian role and national responsibility towards the needy wounded and sick people
