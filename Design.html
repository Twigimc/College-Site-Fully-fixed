Design

Department

The mission of the Interior Design Department is to develop the student’s knowledge of materials, furnishings and construction methods to create environments that meet the commercial and residential needs of clients, by integrating design concepts, space analysis and planning, as well as the perception and realization of the visual, technical, cultural and aesthetic aspects.

Mission

The vision of the department is summarized in the relentless pursuit of excellence in the fields of interior design engineering at various local, regional and even international levels in order to reach excellence in terms of programs and curricula to teach interior design principles for internationally recognized undergraduate and postgraduate degrees, as the Interior Design Department seeks to achieve distinction as a program Pioneering and unique in order to encourage creativity among students of the department in line with the Arab and local culture, in addition to educating them to be pioneering graduates of interior design.

Vision

  • Developing the skills, concepts and training required to practice interior design.
  • Gain knowledge of design history, theories and applications.
  • Developing knowledge of human and behavioral factors related to design programs
  • Development of expertise in the technical aspects of design in general and planning and residential designs in particular
  • Enhancing the student’s knowledge of the human influence on himself, the earth and the climate

FAQ

What is the average grade required for acceptance?

Unspecified grade average for both morning and evening study

What are the branches accepted by the department?

Graduates of the literary branch, Graduates of the biology branch, Graduates of the applied science, Graduates of the scientific branch, graduates from Institute of Fine Arts: Department of Design, graduates of Institute of Applied Arts: Department of Print Design Techniques - Department of Fabric Design Techniques - Department of Garment Manufacturing Techniques from previous years and those born to students from 1990 up for morning studies

What is the corresponding department in government colleges?

The department is twinned with University of Baghdad /College of Fine Arts.

Is there evening study in the department?

Yes, there is an evening study, and admission to it is not included in the age requirement.

How much is the tuition fee?

2,000,000 two million Iraqi dinars

Is the department recognized by the Ministry of Higher Education and Scientific Research?

Yes, all departments of Al-Farabi University College are permanently recognized and listed in the admission guide for private colleges issued by the ministry.
